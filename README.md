🚀 Key Portfolio Projects
This repository serves as a centralized hub for my key business intelligence projects:

[[AirBnB Market Intelligence Dashboard]](https://github.com/Anupam3679/AirBnB-Market-Intelligence-Pricing-Optimization-Dashboard):
Objective: Addressed the volatility in short-term rental income by identifying core drivers of occupancy and pricing power.

Impact: Quantified a 15-20% potential price increase via the "Superhost" premium without loss of occupancy.

Tech: Tableau, SQL, Geospatial Revenue Mapping.

[[E-commerce Sales Analysis]](https://github.com/Anupam3679/-Ecommerce-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard):
Objective: Created a centralized BI tool to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights for regional managers.


Impact: Identified a high correlation between shipping delays and return rates, leading to a revised logistics strategy for Q4.


Tech: Power BI, SQL, Multi-source Data Joining.

[[McDonald's Operational Performance]](https://github.com/Anupam3679/MacDonalds-Sales-Dashboard):
Objective: Designed a decision-support tool for franchise managers to analyze sales trends and maximize ticket size.

Impact: Utilized Pareto Charts to highlight the top 20% of menu items driving 80% of total revenue.

Tech: Tableau, Heat Maps, Transactional Data Cleaning.

[[Spotify Streaming Trends]](https://github.com/Anupam3679/Spotify-Music-Trend-Analysis-): 
Objective: Built an interactive dashboard to analyze streaming trends and artist popularity.

Impact: Engineered custom DAX measures for KPIs including average popularity and stream counts.

Tech: Power BI, DAX, Data Modeling
